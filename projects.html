Minesweeper

(not publicly available yet)

A minimalist command-line Minesweeper game designed for speed, precision, and endurance. No GUI, no distractions — just you and the keyboard.

  • Ultra-lightweight: Runs on anything*. No graphics, no sound, no bloat.
  • Keyboard-only control: Fast, efficient, and perfect for remote sessions or retro hardware.
  • Endless gameplay: Procedurally generated fields give you near-infinite levels.
  • Track your mastery: Detailed explosion/defusion stats to measure your progress and precision.
Whether you're dodging bombs on your grandma's PC or clearing grids over SSH, this game is a pure logic test — raw, focused, and unforgiving.

Ready to defuse the boredom?
