Welcome to our web page!

We are Chromatic Carrot, a small indie game studio from the center of Rotterdam.
Our team is a group of hobbyist game developers. Therefore we make games for joy, not for microtransactions.
When you play our games, it will be available to you full.

We like puzzle games - those are the types that we are making most of the times.
To beat a level, you need to navigate according to a few rules - but in reality it is easier said than executed.

We put our emphasis on the core gameplay than on visuals. This enables our games to run perfectly on older hardware without compromising the gameplay, always offering a never seen before level to play.
Try our games if you like to solve puzzles. If you like what we make, you can support us here.

Thank you for reading this long.
Enjoy your play!
